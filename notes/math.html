费马引理(Fermat's theorem (stationary points))

Let \(f:(a,b)\to R\) be a function and suppose that \(x_0\in (a,b)\) is a point where \(f\) has a local extremum. If \(f\) is differentiable at \(x_0\)

Then \(f'(x_0)=0\)

Proof1: Non-vanishing derivatives implies not extremum

assume without loss of generality that \(K>0\)

$$f'(x_0)=K\text{ means that}$$ $$\lim\limits_{\varepsilon\rarr 0}\frac{f(x_0+\varepsilon)-f(x_0)}{\varepsilon}=K$$

In particular, for sufficiently small \(\varepsilon\) (less than some \(\varepsilon_0\)

$$\frac{f(x_0+\varepsilon)-f(x_0)}{\varepsilon}>K/2$$

If \(\varepsilon>0\), then

$$f(x_0+\varepsilon)>f(x_0)+(K/2)\varepsilon>f(x_0)$$

If \(\varepsilon<0\), then

$$f(x_0+\varepsilon)\lt f(x_0)+(K/2)\varepsilon\lt f(x_0)$$

Thus \(x_{0}\) is not a local or global maximum or minimum of \(f\).

Proof2: Extremum implies derivative vanishes

Suppose that \(x_{0}\) is a local maximum (a similar proof applies if \(x_{0}\) is a local minimum). Then there \(\exists \delta > 0\) such that \((x_0-\delta,x_0+\delta)\sub(a,b)\) and such that we have \(f(x_0)\ge f(x)\) \(\forall x\) with \(|x-x_0|\lt \delta\). Hence for any \(h\in (0,\delta)\) we notice that it holds $$\frac{f(x_0+h)-f(x)}{h}\le0$$ we conclude that \(f'(x_0) \le 0\). On the other hand, for \(h \in (-\delta,0)\) we notice that $$\frac{f(x_0+h)-f(x)}{h}\ge0$$ $$f'(x_0) \ge 0$$ $$f'(x_0)=0$$

罗尔定理

如果：

  1. 函数\(f(x)\)在\([a,b]\)上连续
  2. 函数\(f(x)\)在\((a,b)\)内可导
  3. \(f(a)=f(b)\)

那么：
至少存在一点\(\xi\in (a,b)\)，使得\(f'(\xi)=0\)

证明：设M,m分别为f(x)在[a,b]上的最大值和最小值。
(1)如果\(M=m\)，则\(f(x)\equiv M=m\)。任取任取\(\xi\in (a,b)\)有\(f'(\xi)=0\)
(2)如果\(M\ne m\)，由于\(f(a)=f(b)\)，以此一定有\(f(a)=f(b)\ne M or m\)，不妨设\(f(a)=f(b)\ne M\)，即\(f(x)\)在(a,b)最大值不在端点，因此\(\exists \xi \in (a,b)\)，f(x)在\(x=\xi\)取得最大值，有费马引理，\(f'(\xi)=0\)

拉格朗日中值定理

如果：

  1. 函数\(f(x)\)在\([a,b]\)上连续
  2. 函数\(f(x)\)在\((a,b)\)内可导

那么：

$$f(b)-f(a)=f'(\xi)(b-a)$$ $$f'(\xi)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$$

证明：

$$\text{Let } \phi(x)=f(x)-\left(f(a)+\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}\right)$$ $$\phi(a)=\phi(b)=0$$

由罗尔定理，\(\xi\in (a,b)\)，使得\(\phi'(\xi)=0\)，即

$$f'(\xi)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}$$
